Here's a small lil' list of quotes I love.

Dear Anon, If you every feel like quitting just remember why you started and how far you've come from that part. Ofc, there are times when you go through shit but it doesn't mean that you have to just stay in there and keep on overthinking about it. Skipping for a couple of days (for me) makes me lazy to continue. That's why I learn everyday even if it's a little portion of the thing I'm learning. Do what you think is best for you. Also, don't worry about what others might think of you. Show up everyday and do your best!!

Every. Bit. Counts.
